progemini Color system & product mapping

Afia uses a consistent palette: growth & nature (green), food warmth & energy (terracotta CTA, MENA meals and markets), calm & water (blue for hydration and reminders), and habits (amber for streaks). Calorie and macro rings share the same macro trio; soft red for overages, errors, and AI loading/retry.

Product → color intent
  • Local MENA food DB — warm neutrals for catalog chips; terracotta CTA for “log this meal” from koshari, foul, etc.
  • Daily budgets (BMR/TDEE) — green calorie ring; macro trio: protein=green, carbs=amber, fat=terracotta; weekly views reuse the same tokens.
  • Habits & streaks — amber for chain UI and motivation copy.
  • Hydration — blue ring, widget, reminders vs daily target.
  • AI (plate photo, recipe transformer, smart snacks) — terracotta for primary AI actions; warm surface for review; loading=info blue; error=red with retry affordance.
  • Explore & catalog — category chips color-coded; detail screens use macro colors on ingredients and steps.
  • Manual log — secondary green outline / primary green text (search-off path).
  • Onboarding, auth, tabs (Home, Progress, +, Explore, Account) — brand green for active tab and success path; account/settings stay neutral with green accents where “plan” is referenced.
